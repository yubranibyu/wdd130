Rafting Heroes

Rafting Heroes was born from the passion for the river and adventure of its founder, Yubrani Yaguaramay. Yubrani, an experienced rafting guide, had spent years exploring Colorado rivers and sought to share his knowledge and enthusiasm with others. In 1995, Yubrani founded Rafting Heroes in Pagosa Springs, Colorado, with the goal of offering safe and exciting rafting experiences for people of all ages and abilities. Over the years, Rafting Heroes has grown and become one of the most respected rafting companies in the Western United States. The company has expanded its offerings to include multi-day trips, hiking and camping, and has established partnerships with other local tour operators.

Mission

Ofrecer experiencias de aventura auténticas y seguras que conecten a las personas con la belleza natural de nuestros ríos y comunidades, mientras promovemos la conservación y el respeto por el medio ambiente."
